About

In 2013, one of the co-founders of TopDeck mentioned that he had to visit three different sites to fully design a Magic:
The Gathering proxy deck. He noted the difficulty of finding new cards on Cockatrice, and that he had to visit multiple sites
to first choose his cards, and then to generate proxies for them.

We at TopDeck have come together to build an application that allows you to search for cards, build decks, and generate
proxies all in one place. Among other things, TopDeck offers:

  • Export and import to and from Cockatrice so that you can use your decks online competitively.
  • Choice of proxy art by set per card. Want two types of Mishra's Factory art? TopDeck can do it.
  • Quick and easy generation of proxies inside your browser
  • Tracking of cards in your main deck and in your sideboard.
  • Easy update of the card database for when a new deck comes out.
  • Statistics about your deck.
  • Two color themes so you can feel in your element.

So what does TopDeck need to run?

TopDeck runs on Windows 7, 8, and 10. To see images, generate proxies, and update, TopDeck requires internet. Without
internet, TopDeck will still show you basic information about a card.
